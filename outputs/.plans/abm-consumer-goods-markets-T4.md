# T4: Toolkits, Commercial Applications, and Frontier Directions

## Objective
Research the software platforms used for consumer goods ABMs, any commercial/industry applications, and frontier directions including LLM-powered agents.

## Tasks
1. Search for ABM toolkits used in consumer/marketing models (Mesa, NetLogo, Repast, MASON, AnyLogic, custom)
2. Find commercial applications — CPG companies, consulting firms, marketing agencies using ABMs
3. Look for LLM-powered agents in market simulation (generative agents, GPT-based consumers)
4. Find digital twin applications for consumer markets
5. Identify open challenges: scalability, real-time calibration, interpretability, data requirements

## What to Record
For each important source:
- Title, authors, year, venue, identifier (DOI/arXiv ID/URL)
- Toolkit/platform name and capabilities
- Commercial application domain if applicable
- Whether open-source / reproducible

## Output
Write findings to `outputs/.drafts/abm-consumer-goods-markets-research-T4.md`

## Constraints
- Use paper search (alpha tools) and web search
- Do NOT fetch PDFs or call alpha_get_paper — use abstracts, metadata, and web pages only
- Minimum 5 distinct search queries
