# T1: Core ABM Literature for Consumer Goods Markets

## Objective
Find and summarize the key academic literature on agent-based models applied to consumer goods markets (grocery, FMCG, retail, durable goods, fashion).

## Tasks
1. Search for survey papers and book chapters on ABMs in marketing / consumer markets
2. Identify seminal papers (pre-2015) that established the field
3. Find recent papers (2020-2026) showing current state of the art
4. Look for ABMs specifically in: grocery/FMCG, durable goods, fashion/apparel, online retail
5. Note any meta-analyses or systematic reviews

## What to Record
For each important paper/source:
- Title, authors, year, venue, identifier (DOI/arXiv ID/URL)
- Model type and key contribution
- Market domain (FMCG, durables, etc.)
- Whether it includes empirical validation

## Output
Write findings to `outputs/.drafts/abm-consumer-goods-markets-research-T1.md`

## Constraints
- Use paper search (alpha tools) and web search
- Do NOT fetch PDFs or call alpha_get_paper — use abstracts, metadata, and web pages only
- If only a PDF exists, cite the URL and mark as "full text not parsed"
- Minimum 5 distinct search queries across different angles
