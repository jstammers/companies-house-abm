# Literature Review Plan: UK Housing ABM Literature

**Slug:** uk-housing-abm-literature
**Date:** 2026-04-21
**Objective:** Systematic literature review of agent-based models applied to the UK housing market — covering model architectures, agent types, market mechanisms, calibration approaches, policy experiments, and identified limitations.

---

## Key Questions

1. What are the major UK housing ABMs published to date, who developed them, and how do they relate to each other?
2. What agent types, behavioural rules, and market clearing mechanisms are used across the literature?
3. How are these models calibrated and validated against UK data? What datasets are used?
4. What policy experiments have been conducted (macroprudential, fiscal, monetary) and what were the findings?
5. What stylised facts of UK housing markets can these models reproduce?
6. What are the acknowledged limitations and open research gaps?
7. How does the UK housing ABM literature relate to the broader international housing ABM literature?

## Source Types

- BoE Staff Working Papers (primary: Baptista 2016, Carro 2022, Bardoscia 2024)
- Journal articles (JASSS, JEBO, Journal of Economic Dynamics and Control, Computational Economics)
